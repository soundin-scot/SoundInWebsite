---

A polite founder would announce a product the day they decide to build it. The launch lives in a deck for eighteen months while the engineering team catches up. By the time the thing ships, the original idea has been edited past recognition by investor feedback.

We do the opposite, and we know it costs us. Three of the four cards on [our ecosystem page](/ecosystem) say "coming soon" because they're not done. Holocron is the only one we'll talk about, because Holocron is the only one in production with paying customers.

This is bad marketing. It's also the rule that lets us walk away from a bad spec instead of shipping it. The trade-off is real and we're aware of it. We'd rather show you working software than mood boards.

The one thing we will tell you: if you're on this list, you'll see the next one before anyone else does.

- Cougar
